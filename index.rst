Understanding the UHC UCard
---------------------------
The **UnitedHealthcare UCard** combines your plan details, benefits access, rewards, and payment features into a
single card. Before you can use it, you must activate it online through **activate.uhc.com**.

Key features include:
- Access to Medicare Advantage benefits
- Purchase eligible items with rewards or OTC credits
- Verification for doctor's visits
- Digital card access via the UHC app

Where to Find Your UCard Details
--------------------------------
Before activating your card, keep the following information ready:
- Your **UCard number** (printed on the card)
- Your **Member ID**
- Your **Date of Birth**
- Your ZIP code (linked to your UHC account)

These details are required to verify your identity during activation.

Step 1 — Visit activate.uhc.com
-------------------------------
1. Open any browser on your computer or mobile device.
2. Go to:

   ``https://activate.uhc.com``

3. Allow the page to load fully.
4. Make sure the website address is correct to avoid phishing sites.

Step 2 — Enter Your Card Information
------------------------------------
1. On the activation page, enter your **UCard number** exactly as shown.
2. Enter the **Member ID**.
3. Provide your **Date of Birth**.
4. Enter your **ZIP code**.
5. Click **Continue** to verify your details.

Step 3 — Create or Sign In to Your Account
------------------------------------------
If you already have a MyUHC account:
- Sign in using your username and password.

If you don’t have an account:
- Click **Create Account**.
- Fill out the registration form with your personal details.
- Verify your identity using a 2-step authentication method.

Step 4 — Complete UCard Activation
----------------------------------
1. Review your UCard details.
2. Confirm activation by clicking **Activate Card**.
3. Wait for confirmation — your UCard should now be active.
4. You can now access your digital UCard in the UHC mobile app.

Troubleshooting Activation Problems
-----------------------------------
If you face issues at activate.uhc.com, try the following steps:

**Incorrect Information**
- Double-check your Member ID and UCard number.

**Website Not Loading**
- Clear your browser cache.
- Try a different browser.
- Ensure your internet connection is stable.

**Account Login Problems**
- Click ":forgot password:" or ":forgot username:" on the login page.
- Reset your credentials using your email or phone number.

**Card Not Found Error**
- Make sure you are using the UCard linked to your current plan year.

Using Your UHC UCard After Activation
-------------------------------------
Once activated, your UCard gives you access to:

- **OTC benefits**: Purchase eligible items in-store or online.
- **Reward credits**: Earn and redeem through UHC programs.
- **Doctor visits**: Use the UCard as your plan ID.
- **Digital access**: View card details in the UHC app.

Managing Your UCard & UHC Account
---------------------------------
After activating your card, you can:

- View benefits and remaining balances
- Track reward history
- Replace a lost card
- Update personal information

Visit:

``https://www.myuhc.com``

for full account management.

Security Tips
-------------
- Never share your UCard number publicly.
- Always sign out after using MyUHC.
- Enable 2‑factor authentication for added security.
- Access your card only via official UHC websites.

Final Thoughts
--------------
Activating your UHC UCard at **activate.uhc.com** is a simple process when you have the correct details ready.
Once active, your card becomes your all-in-one access point for benefits, rewards, and Medicare Advantage services.

Stay updated, keep your account secure, and enjoy easy access to your UHC benefits.
